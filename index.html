Why this site exists
Chinese food has been called disgusting on TV, ranked on "worst foods" lists, and reduced to shock content — by people who never understood what they were eating.

WTFeats is a corrective. Every food here gets the explanation it deserves: the history, the chemistry, the craft. No fear-mongering. No exoticism. Just facts.
